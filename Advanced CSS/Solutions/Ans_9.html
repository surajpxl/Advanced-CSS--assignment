Apple Inc.

Apple Inc. is a global leader in technology and innovation, founded in 1976. Headquartered in Cupertino, California, Apple is known for its wide range of consumer electronics, software, and online services.

With iconic products such as the iPhone, iPad, and Mac computers, Apple has consistently pushed the boundaries of technology.

The company's dedication to design excellence and user-friendly interfaces has established it as a pioneer in the tech industry.

Apple's ecosystem, featuring platforms like the App Store and Apple Music, provides a cohesive and integrated user experience, driving its continued success and innovation.
